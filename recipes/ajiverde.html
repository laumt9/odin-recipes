Aji Verde (Peruvian Green Sauce)

Description

Aji verde is a bold and bright Peruvian green sauce. It is spicy and tangy, and packed with fresh flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cups lightly packed fresh cilantro (mostly leaves)
  • 2 medium jalapenos, roughly chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • 1/3 cup grated Cotija or Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. In a food processor or blender, combine all of the ingredients until the cilantro is broken into tiny pieces and sauce is green and smooth.
  2. Taste and adjust if necessary.
  3. Aji verde keeps well in the fridge, covered, for about 1 week.
