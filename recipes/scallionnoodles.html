Scallion Noodles

Description

Soy Scallion Shanghai Noodles, or 葱油拌面, really packs a punch with an intense soy scallion flavor. It is one of the most simple yet so tasty Chinese noodle dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 8 oz scallions, julienned
  • 3 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons light soy sauce
  • 4 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 lb Chinese white noodles (cooked until al dente)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in wok over medium heat, add scallions and let them fry slowly. Remove once golden brown.
  2. To the oil, add both kinds of soy sauce and sugar on low heat until it starts to bubble.
  3. Toss in the noodles, mix and dig in.
